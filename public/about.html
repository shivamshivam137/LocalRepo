About CivicTrack

Our Mission

CivicTrack is dedicated to empowering citizens with verified, accessible, and transparent information about election candidates. We believe that informed voting decisions strengthen democratic processes and create accountability in governance.

What We Do

  • Candidate Verification: We compile and verify detailed information about candidates including education, background, and records.
  • Promise Tracking: We document election promises and monitor their fulfillment status with verified sources.
  • Transparency Reports: We provide verified data on assets, criminal records, and performance metrics.
  • Citizen Engagement: We empower voters to compare candidates and make informed democratic choices.

Our Commitment

CivicTrack is committed to:

  • Accuracy: All information is verified from official sources.
  • Neutrality: We present facts without political bias.
  • Transparency: Our sources and methodologies are always disclosed.
  • Privacy: User data is protected and never sold to third parties.
  • Accessibility: The platform is free and available to all citizens.
